FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What age group is ThinkBlocks for?

ThinkBlocks is designed for children aged 6–11 years, helping them build logic, decision-making, and problem-solving skills.

Do I need the app to play?

No. ThinkBlocks can be played completely offline. The app enhances the experience by unlocking animations, validating answers, and providing instant feedback.

What does the box include?

The box includes 14 interactive card sets, a maze puzzle, 3D shape nets, and a board game designed around decision-making and learning.

How does the app work?

Scan cards or puzzles using the ThinkBlocks app. Correct combinations unlock animations, while incorrect ones guide children to try again.

Are answers provided?

Answers are not directly given. Children are encouraged to think, try, and learn through feedback and community discussions.

Where can I buy ThinkBlocks?

You can buy directly from our website at a discounted price or find ThinkBlocks at selected toy and book stores.
